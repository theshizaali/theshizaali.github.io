Course Description

On completion of this class, students will:

  • Design and implement C programs using the associated development tools.
  • Understand basic means of constructing applications from libraries and communication with services.
  • Demonstrate an understanding of composition as a fundamental design concept for advanced functionalities.
  • Understand representations of programs and processes.
  • Apply UNIX abstractions for processes, process control, inter-process communication, and file systems.

Fundamentally, students will understand that processes are manipulable data, processes control and manage other processes, and applications are composed out of libraries and processes.
