This page is an archive for CS488: Natural Language Processing, taught in Spring 2025 at the University of Washington Bothell.

The course introduced core ideas in natural language processing, including text representation, language modeling, classification, sequence labeling, and neural methods, with attention to real-world applications and responsible design.
