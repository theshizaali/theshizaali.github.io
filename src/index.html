Shiza Ali

I work as a Graduate Student Researcher at the Security Lab SeclaBU in the ECE Department at Boston University, under the advisory of Gianluca Stringhini. During my PhD I have also worked with iDrama Lab and SeclaBU Lab. My research focuses on user-centered security and privacy to design and develop tools to understand how people interact with online systems and mitage risks they migh face online. My expertise lies in the fields of Human-Computer Interaction, Social Computing, Machine Learning, Privacy, and Online Safety.
