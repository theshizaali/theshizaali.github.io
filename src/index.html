Hi 👋🏼 My name is Shiza!

I am a Researcher at The University of Washington where I work within the (RAISE Lab) (Responsibility in AI Systems and Experiences) Lab.
I obtained my PhD in Electrical and Computer Engineering from Boston University where I was a core member of the Security Lab (SeclaBU). I have collaborated with the International Data-driven Research for Advanced Modeling and Analysis Lab (iDrama Lab) and Socio-Technical Interaction Research Lab (STIR LAB).

 

My research focuses on cyber safety, applied machine learning, and social computing. I aim to understand, detect, and combat online harms using a data-driven approach. My research involves identifying pertinent issues within cyberspace, developing tools to mitigate malicious behavior, and offering policymakers guidelines to ensure user safety on social media platforms.
Read more about my research here.

My work has been published in top-tier venues in Online Privacy and Security, Web Measurement and HCI, including ACM CHI, CSCW, IEEE S&P, WebSci, RAIDS and has been reported by WIRED. My research has been supported by the National Science Foundation (NSF) .
